About Neom

NEOM is a futuristic city project in Saudi Arabia, part of the country’s Vision 2030 plan to diversify its economy and reduce dependence on oil. Located in the northwestern region of the country, NEOM aims to be a hub for innovation, sustainability, and cutting-edge technology. The project is designed to include smart cities, ports, entertainment centers, and renewable energy sources, all with a focus on eco-friendly living and advanced infrastructure.
